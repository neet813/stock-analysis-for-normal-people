## The Lessons I Learned

### About Investing
1. **Understanding > Timing** - Know why you're buying
2. **Company Health > Daily Price** - Focus on what matters
3. **Patience > Trading** - 2-3 years beats day trading
4. **Psychology > Magic** - Patterns exist because people believe them
5. **You're Not Dumb** - This stuff IS confusing, that's normal

### About Explaining Complex Things
1. **Start with a question** - "Why do people buy stocks?"
2. **Use analogies** - "It's like a restaurant..."
3. **Show real data** - "Here's actual Apple stock data..."
4. **Be honest** - "This works 65% of time, not 100%"
5. **Test with someone** - If your dad gets it, anyone will
